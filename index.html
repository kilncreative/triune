Beyond the Traditional Drone

Since releasing the first Copper Flute, the number one request I’ve received is for a “drone” version. Creating unique drones is what first pulled me into this craft, so if I was going to do a drone, I wanted it to be more than just placing two bodies together.

I wanted to rethink the existing standards around creating a “drone flute”. My goal was to capture an intentional aesthetic while integrating the beauty of the Copper Flute.

After many tests, prototypes, long nights and early mornings… I am proud to bring you the end result of this vision.

The Copper Flute Triune was created as a work of functional art, combining beautiful design with a harmonious voice. The end result is an instrument like no other.

Today, I am inviting you to be one of the first people in the world to hold a Copper Flute Triune of your own!
