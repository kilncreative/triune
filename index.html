Beyond the Traditional Drone

For years, musicians have requested a "drone version" of the Copper Flute—but simply placing two flute bodies side by side was never going to be enough. The Copper Flute Triune represents a complete reimagining of what a drone instrument can be.

This isn't just another product; it's a unique artistic statement. With three perfectly calibrated resonance chambers working in harmonic unity, the Triune creates a rich tapestry of overtones that transforms solo playing into a full, immersive sonic experience.

Each note you play is supported by three meticulously tuned drone chambers, creating complex harmonic interactions that were previously impossible with traditional drone instruments. The result is an instrument that speaks with multiple voices while remaining intuitive to play.
